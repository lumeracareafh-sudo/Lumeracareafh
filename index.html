About LumeraCare

More Than a Home,
We Are Family.

At LumeraCare AFH LLC, we believe every resident deserves to feel valued, respected, and cared for. Our home was founded by a Civil Engineer and Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA), alongside her husband, a Registered Nurse — bringing together expertise in safe, well-designed living spaces and hands-on medical care. Together, they built a home where every detail supports your loved one's comfort, dignity, and well-being.

What We Offer

Care That Feels Like Home,

At LumeraCare AFH LLC, we go beyond routine care. Every service we provide is rooted in respect, warmth, and a genuine commitment to each resident's quality of life — in a real home, not a facility.

24/7 Personalized Care

With a dedicated caregiver-to-resident ratio, your loved one receives individual attention and support every hour of every day.

Registered Nurse On-Call

Professional medical oversight always within reach — giving families confidence their loved one is in safe, capable hands.

Assistance with Daily Living

Bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility, and transfers — all handled with dignity, patience, and personal care.

Medication Management

Medications administered safely and on schedule, with careful coordination with physicians and healthcare providers.

Home-Cooked Nutritious Meals

Fresh, wholesome meals made daily — adapted to dietary needs, cultural preferences, and personal favorites.

Emergency Call System

Meaningful Activities & Companionship

Gentle exercises, music, arts, and social time — we nurture the mind, body, and spirit every single day.

Housekeeping & Laundry

Open Visitation & Family Connection

Holiday & Birthday Celebrations

Every milestone matters. We mark special moments with joy, warmth, and a true sense of belonging.

Respite & Short-Term Care

Temporary care for families who need relief — your loved one receives the same attentive, compassionate care as long-term residents.

End-of-Life & Hospice Support

Our Specialties

Mental Health Support
